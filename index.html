infoAbout

I've spent over 14 years growing with technology - not by staying in one lane, but by expanding across them with intent.

I began as a Java developer, building a strong foundation in object-oriented programming and clean system thinking. That grounding led me into Android development, where I evolved from Java to Kotlin as the ecosystem matured. During this phase, I was simultaneously contributing to an Angular project - navigating mobile and frontend systems in parallel. Working across layers at the same time strengthened my adaptability, sharpened my ability to switch contexts quickly, and deepened my collaboration with diverse teams.

Over time, my curiosity shifted from building features to understanding the systems behind them. I transitioned back into backend engineering, designing Java-based services and microservices, focusing on scalability, data consistency, and architectural clarity. I expanded into data workflows, strengthened my SQL expertise, and began leveraging Python for data and AI-oriented use cases.

Most recently, I took a deliberate academic pause to complete my M.Sc. in Computer Science. That phase allowed me to revisit and solidify core fundamentals - distributed systems, database internals, consistency models, caching strategies, messaging architectures, authentication systems, containerization, orchestration, and system design principles. It helped me connect practical experience with theoretical depth, strengthening my ability to reason about trade-offs from first principles.

Alongside this, I pursued parallel certifications and structured programs from reputed institutions and respected educators. I completed an AI program from IIT Roorkee and participated in the ByteByteGo AI cohort, further deepening my understanding of modern AI systems and large-scale architectures. These experiences complemented my hands-on background and expanded my perspective on how intelligent systems are designed, scaled, and integrated into real-world products.

Across mobile, frontend, backend, data, distributed systems, and AI, my journey reflects disciplined curiosity and adaptability. I'm comfortable stepping into ambiguity, learning quickly, and operating across technical layers - from writing production code to thinking through architectural decisions.

Today, I approach engineering with a systems mindset. I value clarity over complexity, fundamentals over shortcuts, and sustainable design over quick wins. I enjoy building solutions that are not just functional, but resilient, scalable, and thoughtfully engineered.
